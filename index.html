    About Us



    Sal Zailani started as a barista at an award-winning cafe in the UK in 2012, before becoming its manager within three years. She met Alex Starkovs, a chef with fine dining experience, and moved back to Brunei in 2018 and start Two Sons a cafe named after their two sons.

    Simply some of the best and most thoughtfully presented coffee in Brunei. The owners are true coffee enthusiasts and they keep things fresh with a range of imported beans. If you're interested in third wave coffee and enjoy trying out new beans from around the world, Two Sons is the place to go.
